001

Scalable User Similarity at Netflix Scale

Leiden · 2025 · LSH + MinHash

A memory-efficient pipeline to identify pairs of users with Jaccard similarity > 0.5 across 100K users, 17,770 movies, and 65M+ ratings. Implemented MinHash signatures and LSH banding (b=20, r=5) with MD5 bucketing on SciPy CSR sparse matrices. Came in at ~6 minutes runtime and ~6 GB peak memory against hard limits of 30 minutes and 8 GB — finding ~544 similar pairs consistently.

005

Ransomware Bitcoin Detection at Scale

Nottingham · 2023 · PySpark

Detected ransom-related Bitcoin transactions across 2.9M rows of UCI data (2009–2018) using PySpark for distributed preprocessing and modelling. Handled severe class imbalance (98.6/1.4) with SMOTE-ENN and tuned Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and bagged ensembles via 5-fold CV on macro-F1. The Decision Tree won on accuracy and macro-F1 while staying interpretable enough for high-throughput scoring.
